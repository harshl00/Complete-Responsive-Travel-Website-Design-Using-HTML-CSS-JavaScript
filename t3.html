🌞 Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur – Sightseeing – Jaipur Stay

Meet and Greet by our executives at your disposal. Pickup from railway station. Transfer to hotel & check-in to room. Start Full day sightseeing, Visit the famous tourist hotspot Amer fort, Nahargarh Fort, Jaigarh fort, Kanak Garden, Jal mahal & visit local Jaipur Traditional Market. Back to hotel and overnight stay at hotel in Jaipur. 🌊

🦧 Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing - Jaipur Stay

After breakfast, start your full day local sightseeing tour in Jaipur. Start Jaipur local sightseeing, Visit Alberthall museum, Bidla temple, Hawa Mahal, Jantar mantar, City palace and a stroll around Jaipur pink city to witness vibrant colorful traditions and culture. Back to hotel and overnight stay in Jaipur 🐒

🏝️ Day 3: Udaipur Via Ajmer & Pushkar – Udaipur Stay

Udaipur Via Ajmer & Pushkar – Udaipur Stay After an appetizing breakfast in hotel, check-out and transfer to Udaipur. En-route a full day excursion to Ajmer and Pushkar is planned. Both the cities are culturally vivid, historically royal and have a rich collection of ancient pilgrimage sites. On arrival in Ajmer, have a visit to the famous and prestigiously religious Ajmer-e-Sharif Dargah. Next, visit Adhai-Din-Ka Jhonpara and Ana Sagar Lake. After that, continue your drive to Pushkar. The city houses numerous ancient temples and sacred ghats. Visit ancient temples and Ghats of holy Pushkar Lake. Though the city is home to almost 400 temples but the major attraction is the temple of Lord Brahma. It is the only temple in India dedicated to the worship of Lord Brahma. Continue drive to Udaipur. On arrival, check-in to hotel and overnight stay at Udaipur. 🐠

🌅 Day 4: Udaipur Sightseeing – Departure

After breakfast, check out from hotel. Start your Udaipur city tour. Visit famous tourist hotspot Fateh Sagar, Nehru Garden set amidst Fatehsagar, Maharana Pratap Memorial, Sahelion-KI-Bari, Sukhadia Circle (Drive Pass), karni mata temple and Bhartiya Lok kala Museum where a rich collection of ornaments, folk dresses and puppets is showcased. Next, Visit City Palace of Udaipur which enjoys hordes of tourists due to its exquisite design and grandeur. The palace is situated on the bank of Lake Pichola which imparts the most beautiful sights. Visit Gulab Bagh and Jagdish Mandir. In evening, drop you at Udaipur railway station or airport for onward journey. 🏯
